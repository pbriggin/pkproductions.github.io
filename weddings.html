Weddings

PK Productions is committed to providing an affordable, high quality memory of your big day. We pride ourselves on our "fly on the wall approach" - instead of remembering a camera in your face as you say your "I do," you remember your spouse's face.

We offer multiple packages, ranging from a simple capture of the ceremony to a comprehensive highlight reel of the entire day and preparation. See below for details! And please don't let price be a showstopper - we believe everyone deserves a chance to capture such an incredible moment, and are happy to work with you.

Ceremony Only

$300 | This includes filming and editing of your ceremony in UHD from multiple angles, as well as HD audio recording.

Ceremony & Reception

$500 | This includes filming and editing of your ceremony and reception in UHD from multiple angles, as well as HD audio recording. Our most popular package.

Ceremony, Reception, & Highlight Reel

$1,400 | The "reel" deal (get it). Our comprehensive package includes a the ceremony and recpetion package as well as a full coverage of your day - from getting ready to drone shots of the venue, to smooth b-roll of the event space.
